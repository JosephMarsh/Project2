About Me.

Personality

have have a love for science, reason and math (not that I’m great at them mind you). I have a good sense of humor and I’m told I’m pretty funny. I work in IT full time and go to school full time so I don’t get to game as much as I’d like anymore. But I’m a huge fan of the pen and paper form of role playing game. Professional

I've spent most of my life in the Army. I was originally tricked into becomeing a telephone operator. The MOS was called “Computer Network Switching Systems Operator”. Luckily I was only disappointed in this choice for about 2 years. Then the Army trained me to be one of the very first Operators of their new IP based telephone switching systems. I learn about networking, sub-netting, codecs and all kinds of useful concepts and skills in today’s technology hungry world. Eventually I became a team chief, before leaving the service with 11 years under my belt.
